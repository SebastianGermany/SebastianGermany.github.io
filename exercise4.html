Hook Head Lighthouse

Explore Ireland’s Ancient Maritime Landmark

Guided Lighthouse Tours

Step inside one of the world’s oldest operational lighthouses and discover its fascinating history. Knowledgeable guides will bring to life stories of shipwrecks, heroic rescues, and centuries of maritime tradition.

Climb the winding staircase to the balcony for breathtaking panoramic views of the rugged coastline and the wild Atlantic Ocean stretching as far as the eye can see.

Visitor Centre & Café

Enjoy a warm welcome at the modern visitor centre, where interactive exhibits explain the unique heritage of Hook Head. Learn about the lighthouse’s origins in the 12th century and its role in guiding sailors safely to shore.

Relax after your tour at the café, which serves freshly prepared food, snacks, and delicious coffee, all while you admire spectacular sea views through the large windows.

Coastal Walks & Wildlife

Take a leisurely stroll along the dramatic cliffs surrounding Hook Head. The walking trails offer a chance to spot seabirds, seals, and even dolphins in the waters below.

In spring and summer, the headland blooms with wildflowers, creating a colourful backdrop for photography, picnics, and moments of quiet reflection by the sea.
